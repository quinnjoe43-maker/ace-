FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you call.

Do you charge a call-out fee?+
No — Ace Plumbing York does not charge a call-out fee. We charge hourly rates with a one-hour minimum, so you only pay for the work done.
Do your prices include VAT?+
No VAT is added to any of our rates. The price you're quoted is the price you pay — straightforward and transparent.
How quickly can you come out?+
For emergencies we aim to respond as quickly as possible. For planned work we'll arrange a time that suits you. Call Nicky directly for availability.
Are you Gas Safe registered?+
Yes, fully. Gas Safe registration is a legal requirement for any engineer working on gas appliances in the UK. You can verify our registration at gassaferegister.co.uk.
What is a CP12 certificate?+
A CP12 is the landlord gas safety certificate required by UK law. It must be renewed annually and provided to tenants within 28 days of the check. We offer these at a fixed price of £70 including up to 2 appliances.
Do you work on back boilers?+
Unfortunately we don't service back boilers for CP12 certificates or boiler services, but we do carry out other work. Give us a call to discuss your specific situation.
Can you give a quote over the phone?+
Absolutely — and it's always free. Call Nicky on 07737 761984 and describe the job. He'll give you an honest estimate straight away, no obligation.
